Enchanted Kingdom

Location

This place is located in Santa Rosa, Laguna, Philippines. Enchanted Kingdom is the Philippine's biggest amusement park, offering exciting rides, interactive shows, and themed activities for all ages. It has seven themed zones which include thrilling rides like the Space Shuttle and EKstreme Drop Tower, as well as kid-friendly rides like the Grand Carousel and Rio Grande Rapids. Visitors can also enjoy shows, 4D experiences, and yearly festivities, making it an ideal spot for enjoyment and adventure.

History

Enchanted Kingdom was built in 1995 and is owned and operated by Enchanted Kingdom Inc., founded by businessman Cesar Mario Mamon. As the country's first and largest amusement park, it remains a top destination for families.

Why do I love this place?

I love this place because, since I was a kid, my friends and family would always go to Enchanted Kingdom. It holds a special place in my heart because it’s where I’ve felt my happiest, creating unforgettable memories with the people I love. The first time we visited was in 2012—I was only 7, and my sister was 5. Our first ride together was the Boulderville Express, the only ride we were allowed on without being accompanied. The second picture was taken in 2023 when I went with my friends to celebrate our birth month. Enchanted Kingdom offers a free pass for birthday celebrants, making it the perfect place for a fun reunion. Since we don’t get to see each other often, that day was extra special to me. No matter how many times I visit, this place will always feel like home.
